Cluster Reset Procedure:

  • With the key OFF, plug in the charge cord and wait for the charge indicator to turn green.
  • Wait 10 seconds, turn the key ON, wait 10 seconds, then turn the key OFF.
  • Unplug the cart and turn the key ON. The gauge should reset to 100%.
  • If this doesn’t work, contact your dealer to replace the gauge cluster.
